3.0 SAP Cloud

SAP HANA Cloud Platform is an open platform-as-a-service that provides unique in-memory database and application services. It is the proven cloud platform that enables you to rapidly develop new applications or extend existing ones. Enabling anyone to extend SAP applications in minutes, all in the cloud.

SAP HANA Cloud Platform is a platform-as-a-service for extending, integrating and building apps to meet new challenges, attract new customers, and drive new business. We are experts in moving you on premise solutions to cloud and also integrating the same with Cloud Identity and with Social Media authentication.

3.1 SAP Cloud Identity

We specialize in SAP Cloud Identity Solution to enable SAP Single Sign-On (SSO) & Social Authentication* for your on-premise, on-cloud or hybrid solutions.

Offering – Implement SSO

What is SSO – Authenticate once and subsequently access SAP and non-SAP application in a secure and user friendly way. Meet company and regulatory requirements. Improve security measures and protect the company.

Solve security and compliance issue – Only one secure password to remember. Only one password to store and protect. Automated login while you work.

3.2 SAPBusiness By Design
Cloud-based ERP for mid-market companies and subsidiaries

Manage your entire business with a single cloud ERP solution – SAP Business ByDesign. Ideally suited for upper mid-market companies and subsidiaries of large corporations, this complete and integrated SaaS suite supports financials, human resources, sales, procurement, customer service, supply chain management, and more.
