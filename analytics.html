In order to be remarkable and run better, you should act faster and predict faster. If you want to make any quick decision or if you want to know complete info of your business at your fingertips, here comes Synapse Analytics Solutions.

By combining SAP's tools with our functional and industry expertise, Synapse delivers Analytics solution that help everyone in the organization to minimize risk, maximize shareholder value, reduce costs and ultimately run better.

Organizations are challenged to create value using data and turn it into opportunities. Analytics solutions from SAP offers a comprehensive – yet simple – integrated platform that helps unlock the real value in your data for smarter decisions making. The rewards are significant for those who successfully leverage analytics : stretching their competitive advantage, driving and improving lives. We offer Business Intelligence and Big data, BODS (Business Objects and data Services) tools they need to make faster, more informed decisions. Our BI software and solutions – including ad HOC reporting and self-service data visualizations and dashboards, predictive analytics, data cleansing and mobile apps – can help business users improve performance and become more effective in everything they do. We also offer Migration and conversions (Extract data from multiple source systems and load data into target systems)

With more and more companies worldwide turning into truly global operations, disseminating the right information, to the right person, in the right format, and within the right timeframe is critical to managing the business effectively.In addition to streamlining the operations and enhancing productivity, business information is an integral part of all sales, marketing, and technology initiatives that decides the future direction of the organization.

Synapse brings together a deep understanding of the business and technology dimensions of an organization to harness information to improve decision-making, financial management, regulatory compliance and customer service.Our suite of information strategy, data warehousing, data mining and information analytics services measure, analyze and optimize business performance across the entire enterprise.Our Business Intelligence practice provides BI and Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) solutions in the areas of reporting, planning and consolidation.

2.1 Business Intelligence

Whether you are just getting started with analytics or have invested in and implemented one or more analytics solutions, our Analytics Advisors are your coach for both strategic and technology activities. While supporting you to be an analytics-driven organization, our services help you to with the - Identification of strategic business drivers and change-management needs in order to define the analytics vision and strategy- Delivery of a custom road map to determine the analytics target architecture- Creation of an analytics governance model to establish standards and processes for setup, adoption and use.

Key benefit include tie business strategy to actionable analytics initiatives; understand and prioritize when and where to spend constrained budget; identify ways to harvest existing analytics investments; drive analytics capability that supports business strategy; build long-term analytics plan based on expertise and insights specific to analytics solutions; identify areas of opportunity for minimizing deployment risks and maximizing cost efficiency; simplified architecture and operations, higher reusability, thus lower TCO.

We specialize in SAP BW Suite, Business Objects Suite, SAP Lumira, Cloud for Analytics and Digital Boardroom among others. We specialize and approach this in below steps:

  3. Data Mining
  4. Visualization

  • Deploy and manage thousands of predictive data sets and models cost effectively

We empower business users of all skills to better understand data - so they can turn that data into compelling and engaging stories for specific audiences. With intuitive data visualization solutions from SAP, your business users can quickly and easily:

  • Combine data from multiple sources
  • Visualize data and analyze trends
  • Share infographics and data stories
  • Deliver critical information on mobile devices

While we specialize in multiple visualization tools our emphasis is on SAP Lumirawhich lets you combine multiple data sources to get the big picture and granular details together. Using beautiful visualizations and a drag and drop interface, explore and analyze outliers and trends to maximize your data knowledge.
